What is the American Gem Society™?

The American Gem Society™ (AGS) was established by early pioneers in the jewelry industry over 70 years ago. The American Gem Society is a not-for-profit trade association of fine jewelers, designers and appraisers in North America. AGS members are dedicated to consumer protection, ethical business practices, and the development of superior gemological skills and knowledge.

Today the American Gem Society™ is comprised of nearly 3,500 jewelers, retailers, suppliers, individual titleholders: Registered Jewelers, Certified Gemologists®, and Certified Gemologist Appraisers, and Independent Certified Appraisers – the elite of the jewelry industry.
